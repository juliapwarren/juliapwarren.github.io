Hi! I'm Julia

Publishing professional with five years of experience in scientific communication.

Education and experience

I recently earned a Master of Arts in English with a concentration in Technical and Professional Communication from East Carolina University. Prior to entering the world of publishing, I attended UNC Chapel Hill and graduated with a BA in sociology and a minor in chemistry. For a while, I thought I may be a pharmacist, so I have a science background! That makes it easier for me to make sense of scientific papers.

Personal
